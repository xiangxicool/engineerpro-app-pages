Engineer Master Pro Support

Engineer Master Pro is a paid native iOS utility with five engineering calculators, local history, and copyable outputs.

Main Test Path

  1. Open Engineer Master Pro.
  2. Go to Dashboard and open one calculator module.
  3. Enter two values and tap Calculate.
  4. Tap Copy or Save to History.
  5. Open History to review saved results.
  6. Open Settings to review privacy and support links.

Support Notes

No login, subscription, in-app purchase, cloud upload, media download, external payment, analytics, advertising, or tracking is used.

Privacy

Engineer Master Pro processes entries locally on device. See the privacy policy for full data-practice details.
