StemSheet Support

StemSheet is a paid native iOS utility for stem delivery worksheets, track counts, group planning, revision notes, and copyable handoff reports.

Main Test Path

  1. Open StemSheet.
  2. Enter stem count, group count, revision notes, and delivery context.
  3. Review checklist sizing, handoff status, and revision risk.
  4. Save the delivery snapshot or copy the handoff report.

Support Notes

No login, subscription, in-app purchase, cloud upload, media download, external payment, analytics, advertising, or tracking is used.

Privacy

StemSheet processes entries locally on device. See the privacy policy for this app for the full data-practice summary.
