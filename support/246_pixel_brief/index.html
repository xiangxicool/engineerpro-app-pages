PixelBrief Support

PixelBrief is a paid native iOS utility for image delivery briefs, canvas requirements, asset counts, aspect notes, and copyable production reports.

Main Test Path

  1. Open PixelBrief.
  2. Enter canvas dimensions, asset counts, aspect notes, and delivery context.
  3. Review megapixel estimate, workload count, and production guidance.
  4. Save the delivery brief or copy the asset report.

Support Notes

No login, subscription, in-app purchase, cloud upload, media download, external payment, analytics, advertising, or tracking is used.

Privacy

PixelBrief processes entries locally on device. See the privacy policy for this app for the full data-practice summary.
