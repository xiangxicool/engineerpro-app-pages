CueTrim Support

CueTrim is a paid native iOS utility for planning audio trim cue sheets, segment timing, source remainders, and copyable export notes.

Main Test Path

  1. Open CueTrim.
  2. Enter the source duration, trim points, and cue notes.
  3. Review segment duration and source remainder results.
  4. Save the cue entry or copy the report for your project notes.

Support Notes

No login, subscription, in-app purchase, cloud upload, media download, external payment, analytics, advertising, or tracking is used.

Privacy

CueTrim processes entries locally on device. See the privacy policy for this app for the full data-practice summary.
