Engineer Master Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Engineer Master Pro is a paid native iOS utility that includes five engineering calculators, local history, and copyable results.

Data Collection

Engineer Master Pro does not collect, upload, sell, share, track, or analyze personal data.

User Content

Input values, calculated results, and saved history remain on the user's device unless the user chooses to share copied output with iOS system tools.

Permissions

The app does not request camera, microphone, photo library, location, contacts, local network, or tracking permission.

Third Parties

The app does not include advertising, analytics, tracking, external payment, login, cloud sync, or third-party SDKs.

Payment Model

The app is sold as a paid download. It does not include subscriptions, in-app purchases, external payment links, license keys, or manual unlock flows.

Contact

For support, use the Engineer Master Pro support page.
