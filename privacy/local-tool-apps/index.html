Local Tool Apps Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

This privacy policy applies to local utility apps published by this developer when the App Store product page links to this policy. These apps are designed to perform their core workflows on the user's device without an account, analytics, advertising, tracking, or cloud processing.

Data Collection

The covered apps do not collect, upload, sell, share, track, or analyze personal data.

User Content

Values, notes, calculations, reports, generated text, saved history, and other app entries remain on the user's device unless the user chooses to copy, export, or share them with iOS system tools.

Permissions

The covered apps do not request camera, microphone, photo library, location, contacts, local network, Bluetooth, calendar, reminders, health, or tracking permission unless the individual App Store product page clearly states otherwise.

Third Parties

The covered apps do not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, third-party login, external payment, or cloud sync services.

Payment Model

When an app is sold as a paid download, its core features are available after purchase. The covered apps do not use external payment links, license keys, manual unlock flows, or non-Apple payment systems for digital features.

Children's Privacy

The covered apps are general-purpose utilities and are not directed to children. They do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated when app behavior changes. If an app begins using account features, permissions, cloud services, third-party SDKs, or data collection, its App Store privacy details and policy link will be updated accordingly.

Support

For product support, use the support page linked from the App Store listing.
