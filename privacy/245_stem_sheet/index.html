StemSheet Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

StemSheet is a paid native iOS utility for stem delivery worksheets, track counts, group planning, revision notes, and copyable handoff reports.

Data Collection

StemSheet does not collect, upload, sell, share, track, or analyze personal data.

User Content

Stem delivery notes, handoff worksheets, saved snapshots, and copied reports remain on the user's device unless the user chooses to share them with iOS system tools.

Permissions

The app does not request camera, microphone, photo library, location, contacts, local network, or tracking permission.

Third Parties

The app does not include advertising, analytics, tracking, external payment, login, cloud sync, or third-party SDKs.

Payment Model

The app is sold as a paid download. It does not include subscriptions, in-app purchases, external payment links, license keys, or manual unlock flows.

Contact

For support, use the StemSheet support page.
