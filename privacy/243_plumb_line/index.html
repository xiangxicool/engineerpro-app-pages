PlumbLine Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

PlumbLine is a paid native iOS utility for manual vertical offset notes, tolerance checks, correction planning, and copyable install reports.

Data Collection

PlumbLine does not collect, upload, sell, share, track, or analyze personal data.

User Content

Manual alignment measurements, tolerance notes, saved snapshots, and copied reports remain on the user's device unless the user chooses to share them with iOS system tools.

Permissions

The app does not request camera, microphone, photo library, location, contacts, local network, or tracking permission.

Third Parties

The app does not include advertising, analytics, tracking, external payment, login, cloud sync, or third-party SDKs.

Payment Model

The app is sold as a paid download. It does not include subscriptions, in-app purchases, external payment links, license keys, or manual unlock flows.

Contact

For support, use the PlumbLine support page.
